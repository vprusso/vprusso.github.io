In a comprehensive review paper on quantum error mitigation by Cai et al.
([arXiv:2210.00921](https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.00921)), the authors posed an open problem: what is the full landscape of
the "zoo" of QEM techniques?

![qem-open-problem](/blog/qem_open_problem.png)

I recently launched a website to help address this: the [QEM Zoo](https://qemzoo.com/).

It's a living catalog of quantum error mitigation and suppression techniques, inspired by the [Complexity
Zoo](https://complexityzoo.net/) and the [Error Correction Zoo](https://errorcorrectionzoo.org/). The site organizes
techniques into protocols, supporting methods, noise models, and applications—all searchable and filterable.

Contributions welcome: [github.com/vprusso/qemzoo](https://github.com/vprusso/qemzoo)
